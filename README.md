# **Pico y Placa program**

This program takes as an input:
- license plate number
- A date
- Time of the day

## **Rules of pico y placa**
Hours that you are not allowed:
- 7:00 am -9:30 am
- 16:00 pm - 19:30 pm
### License plate number ended in:

- Monday 1 and 2 
- Tuesday 3 and 4
- Wednesday 5 and 6
- Thrusday 7 and 8
- Friday 9 and 0
- Saturday and Sunday: everybody can drive
